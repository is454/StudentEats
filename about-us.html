About Us

Welcome to our page! We are a team of two college students: Isabella Sarmiento Mejia and Noor Farouqui that prefer home cooked meals.

We are passionate about eating good food while sticking to a student budget.

Our Mission: Helping Students Eat Better

We're not just about cooking—we're about empowering students to eat smarter, feel better, and enjoy the process. Whether you're in a dorm or your first apartment, StudentEats is here to make meals fun, healthy, and budget-friendly.

Our History: From Takeout to Take Charge

As students, we found ourselves constantly spending money on takeout and convenience foods. Over time, not only did it hurt our wallets, but it also led to unhealthy eating habits and weight gain. We knew something had to change. That’s when we started experimenting with easy, affordable home-cooked meals—and StudentEats was born.
