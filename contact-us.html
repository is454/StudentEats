Frequently Asked Questions

Can I substitute ingredients in the recipe?

Yes! Many recipes are flexible. Common substitutions include using Greek yogurt instead of sour cream, almond milk instead of dairy milk, or gluten-free flour blends for regular flour. Always consider how the swap might affect taste or texture.

How do I store leftovers?

Most leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for 3–5 days. For longer storage, consider freezing. Be sure to let hot dishes cool before sealing and refrigerating.

Can I double or halve the recipe?

Absolutely. Most recipes can be scaled up or down. Just adjust cooking times accordingly—smaller portions may cook faster, while larger ones may need more time.
