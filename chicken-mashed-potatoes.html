Servings: 2 people for 3 days   |   Cook Time: ~30 minutes

üßÇ Ingredients

Chicken

  • 3 large chicken breasts
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, Italian seasoning
  • 1 lemon

Mashed Potatoes

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Milk
  • Salt, pepper

Asparagus

  • 1 bunch of asparagus
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil (or preferred oil)

Broccoli

  • 2 heads of broccoli
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, smoked paprika
  • Olive oil (or preferred oil)

üë©‚Äçüç≥ Instructions

1. Start by prepping your chicken. Clean your chicken with water or lemon, remove fatty pieces, and butterfly it into six thin slices.
2. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, lemon, and garlic powder. Let it rest while you make the mashed potatoes.
3. Peel the potatoes, cut them into medium-sized pieces, and boil them in salted water.
4. While the potatoes boil, prepare your veggies: For broccoli, place it on a baking sheet with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and smoked paprika. For asparagus, drizzle with olive oil, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
5. Roast the vegetables in the oven or air fryer for 10 minutes or until desired doneness. Keep an eye on them!
6. Once the potatoes are soft, strain the water and mash them, adding milk until you achieve a fluffy consistency, then season with salt and pepper.
7. Cook the chicken in a pan with olive oil, and serve everything together. Enjoy your meal prep!
