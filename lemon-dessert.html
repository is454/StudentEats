Servings: 18 people   |   Cook Time: ~30 minutes + 1 hour chill time

üßÇ Ingredients

  • 1 packet of biscuits
  • 3 lemons
  • 2 limes
  • 1 can of cream
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk

üë©‚Äçüç≥ Instructions

1. In a medium-sized bowl, pour in the cream and sweetened condensed milk. Mix well.
2. Squeeze the juice of the lemons and limes into the mixture. Stir thoroughly. Add more lemon juice if desired for extra tanginess.
3. In a tray, lay down a layer of biscuits.
4. Spread enough of the lemon mixture over the biscuits to fully cover them.
5. Repeat layering biscuits and lemon mixture until all of the mixture is used, ending with the lemon mixture as the final top layer.
6. Garnish the top with lime zest.
7. Let the dessert rest in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving. Enjoy!
