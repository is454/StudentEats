Servings: 2 people for 3 days   |   Cook Time: 30 minutes

üßÇ Ingredients

Steak

  • Flank Steak
  • 1 inch of fresh ginger
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • White Pepper
  • Mirin
  • Honey
  • Sesame oil

Broccoli

  • 2 heads of broccoli
  • Rice

üë©‚Äçüç≥ Instructions

1. Start by cleaning your steak with water and pat it down. Cut thin bite-size pieces and place them into a large bowl.
2. Mince the garlic and ginger. Add each into the bowl with ¬º cup of soy sauce, 2 tablespoons of sesame oil, 2 tablespoons of honey, white pepper, black pepper, 1 teaspoon of mirin, and salt to taste.
3. Let this sit while you prepare your broccoli and rice. Start by boiling two pans with water. Cut both broccoli heads and place the broccoli into the boiling water with salt. Blanch the broccoli.
4. Pour a mug of rice into a bowl and start rinsing it with water until the water is clear. Once clear, add it to the boiling water. The water should be a finger higher than the rice.
5. While the rice is boiling and the broccoli is cooking, in a large pan, start cooking the rest of your ginger and garlic with some chili flakes if you like spice. Once it is aromatic, add your steak and cook until golden brown, then add your broccoli.
6. Once the water is dry from the rice, cover it with a lid and lower the heat to low. Then, once the water is completely dry, your rice is ready to serve.
7. Serve the steak with some steamed rice and enjoy!
